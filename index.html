ionic-color-picker

I am a color picker component for the Ionic Framework.

You can use me if you need the users of your ionic application to choose a color from rgb, hsl, hsv color spaces or just from a predefined list of colors, including css named colors.

I am very easy to use, as I am based on just one ng-model aware angularjs directive.
